What is sleep debt?

Sleep debt is the cumulative difference between your biological sleep need and actual sleep. Over consecutive nights, deficits impair cognitive function, immune response, and emotional regulation. Even small nightly shortfalls add up quickly.

Clinical insight: One week of 6-hour nights creates a reaction-time delay comparable to a 0.05% blood alcohol concentration.
⚡

How to recover from sleep debt fast

Extend sleep 1-2 extra hours nightly 20-minute power naps before 3 PM Morning sunlight for circadian reset No caffeine after 2 PM Consistent bedtime even on weekends

Research indicates each hour of debt may require 1.5 hours of extra rest. Your personalized plan below adapts to your specific deficit level.
